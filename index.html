Alex Dean

I'm a software engineer at Amazon Web Services. I'm interested in large-scale distributed machine learning systems. I studied computer science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I live in Seattle.

At AWS, I work on managing the exabyte-scale infrastructure for EBS Snapshots and extending snapshot availability to various local zones. Previously, I worked on ML infra at Capital One. I strongly believe in the power of self-directed personal projects to expand your engineering abilities. For more information, see my projects and my GitHub.

I'm also interested in the impact of communication technologies on modern society, particularly on collective attention, self-awarness, and political discourse. You can see my thoughts and writings on this topic at my Substack.

In my spare time, I enjoy doing mathematics and solving interesting problems. I'm broadly interested in theoretical computer science, particularly graph-related algorithm and optimization problems, as well as analytic number theory. For more information, see my research and my Google Scholar.
